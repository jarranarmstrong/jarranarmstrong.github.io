Jarran Ahaz Armstrong is a multi-instrumentalist musician and composer from Church Hill, Tennessee. Known for his chill instrumentals, soothing vocals, and dream-like lyricism, he manages to create a unique and gripping listening experience with every release, blending influences of Hip-Hop, R&B, Blues, Classical, Metal, Rock, Jazz, and EDM (to name a few).

In 2016, he began releasing music independently through SoundCloud with his first single Praises, where he gained the attention of his classmates and others throughout his town. Following this release were other tracks with similar styles such as Mixed Emotions, Southern Classic, Wait, and Mic Skills.

As Jarran moved on to releasing music to major streaming services, his sound changed as well. Marking this was the release of Cold Thoughts In An Open Mind, a bare-bones acoustic guitar song with inspiration coming more from artists like Elliott Smith than his usual hip-hop roots. Releases that followed this development (such as Felt Tongues, Bend To Your Will, Life Is Fine!, and Caslenia) would do more to cement the sound that he is commonly associated with.

In addition to his independently produced singles, Jarran composes choral music regularly, which is often programmed and performed by his school's concert choir. He has been a part of several regional and state level choral events as well as local choir festivals with his his high school choir.
